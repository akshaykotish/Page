Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the dashboard? ▼
Visit akshaykotish.com/dashboard and log in with your registered phone number. Only authorized users can access the dashboard.
How do I pay an invoice? ▼
Click the payment link in the email you received, or log in to the dashboard and navigate to your bills. Payments are processed securely via Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking, wallets).
Can I get a copy of my invoice? ▼
Yes. Log in to the dashboard, go to Billing, and click "Preview" on any invoice. You can print it or save as PDF. You can also request it by emailing us.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and wallets via Razorpay. Bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS) are also accepted — bank details are on the invoice.
How do I integrate with the API? ▼
Visit the API Documentation for endpoints, authentication, and examples. You'll need an API key — request one from your administrator.
I have a billing dispute. What do I do? ▼
Email us at akshaykotish@gmail.com with your invoice number and a description of the issue. We'll respond within 24 hours.
